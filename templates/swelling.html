Swelling
Swelling is one of the first symptoms of IV failure, however it can be difficult to detect visually. Swelling occurs due to a buildup of fluid under the skin and inflammation due to contact with the infiltrating medicine. IV-Safe detects swelling by utilizing a material that changes color upon stretching. The material is objective, unlike a visual assessment. It will be able to detect even small swelling changes localized to the IV site.
